Allergen Information

At OrgaincMe Cafe, we understand the importance of dietary and allergen concerns. Please be informed of the following:

Milk and Dairy Products: Our menu offers a variety of beverages and pastries that contain milk and other dairy products. For dairy-free alternatives, we provide options such as almond milk, soy milk, and oat milk.

Nuts and Nut Derivatives: Some of our products, especially certain pastries and specialty drinks, may contain nuts or are prepared in an environment where nuts are processed.

Gluten: Items like sandwiches, pastries, and cakes may contain gluten. We strive to offer gluten-free alternatives, which are clearly marked on our menu.

Soy: Several of our beverages and food items contain soy or soy-based ingredients.

We take allergies and dietary preferences seriously and maintain strict cross-contamination policies, but please note that we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment. If you have a specific allergy or dietary requirement, please inform our staff before ordering, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

For any further questions or concerns about allergen information, please do not hesitate to contact our staff.
