A Day in the Life of an Organic ME Beekeeper

Author: Sarah(CEO)

Posted On: 21/11/2023


Intro

Welcome to the Organic ME blog! Today, I want to take you behind the scenes of one of the most fascinating and crucial aspects of our operation – beekeeping. As a beekeeper at Organic ME, my days are filled with the buzzing of bees, the sweet aroma of honey, and the profound satisfaction of working in harmony with nature.


Morning Rituals: Greeting the Bees

My day starts early in the morning, just as the sun begins to light up the beautiful Borders region of Scotland. The first thing I do is check on our bee colonies. This involves a careful inspection to ensure the health and well-being of our bees. I look for signs of activity, check their food supplies, and make sure there are no threats to their habitat. The peaceful hum of the bees and the fresh morning air is an invigorating way to start the day.


Midday: The Art of Honey Extraction

By midday, it's time to extract honey. This is a delicate process that requires a gentle touch. I suit up in my beekeeping gear and begin the extraction process. Our approach is sustainable, ensuring that we leave enough honey for the bees while collecting the excess. The honey from Organic ME is pure, unfiltered, and has a unique flavor that reflects the diverse flora of our region.


Afternoon: Education and Community Engagement

In the afternoons, I often engage in community activities. This could involve hosting a workshop for local school children, educating them about the importance of bees to our ecosystem, or leading a session in our Organic Gardening course, where I share insights on creating bee-friendly gardens. It's fulfilling to see the community's interest in organic and sustainable practices grow.


Evening: Reflecting and Planning

As the day winds down, I spend some time reflecting on the day’s work and planning for the next. Beekeeping is not just about the here and now; it’s about looking ahead, understanding the cycles of nature, and ensuring that we are doing our part to maintain the balance.


Conclusion: A Rewarding Journey

Being a beekeeper at Organic ME is more than a job; it's a journey filled with learning, challenges, and rewards. Each day, I'm reminded of the impact we have on our environment and the responsibility we carry. Our honey is a testament to the dedication and care we put into every aspect of our work.

I hope this glimpse into a day in my life as a beekeeper gives you a better understanding of the love and effort that goes into every jar of Organic ME honey. Remember, when you choose organic, you're not just choosing a product; you're supporting a way of life that respects and nurtures our planet.
